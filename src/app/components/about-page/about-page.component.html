About Me

I am Colombian-American

Raised in Florida, the sunshine state.
I now live in Ann Arbor, where I now go to school at the University of Michigan.

At the University of Michigan, I am encouraged to combine my academic interests and find career paths that allow me to express my curiosity.
Ultimately, this led me to change my initial thoughts on only pursuing a degree in Computer Science and to pursue a double major instead.
After discovering Cognitive Science with an emphasis in computation it felt like an ideal second major for me.
Through my Cognitive Science major, I embrace interdisciplinary study. I see the brain as nature's best computer, and how we can learn from the brain to develop intelligent computation.
Recently, I have become curious about applying machine learning methods that derive from human cognition to non-traditional use cases.
