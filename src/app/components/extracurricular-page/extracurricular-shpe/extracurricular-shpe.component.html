SHPE

The goal of this chapter is to foster a sense of community among Hispanic engineering and applied sciences students at the University of Michigan and other SHPE student chapters. Core values are diversity, equity, inclusion, and familia.

Vice President Internal

Elected to be Vice President Internal for our chapter for the 2020-2021 school year. Under this role I am in charge of internal affairs of our chapter, notably our Academic, Community and Outreach Programs. I also oversee a LeaderSHPE Chair who leads the LeaderSHPE Program which helps give underclassmen an opportunity to grow their leadership skills.

For our Academic logistics I oversee two Academic chairs where we host a number of programs to help foster academic success of our members. Some examples are Study Tables with SHPE Grad, Academic resource compilations, and mentorship programs.

In terms of Outreach, I oversee an Outreach Chair where we plan events for community service and empowering hispanics in our area. Our biggest events are an annual Noche de Sciencias and Student Panels for Incoming First-year and High School Students.

An important part of my role is building community and a safe place for members to collaborate and grow toward each other's success. I plan social events to foster member bonding and express our Hispanic roots, such as Bachata con Horchata and Salsa con Salsa.

LeaderSHPE Scholar

As a sophomore I applied to be a part of the LeaderSHPE program, I was selected to be a part of the program alongside 6 other SHPE members. This program is focused on giving underclassmen an opportunity to to learn about all the internal logistics of the chapter to prepare us to be either a chair or board member in the future.

During this time, we planned events such as Globalbonanza and Winter Bash. Our plans were shared with the core board and we used feedback and their resources to help make our plans a reality.

SHPE Jr. Treasurer

Position I held for two years under the SHPE Jr. chapter at my high school. Ensured financial reports, reimbursements, invoices, project approvals, and purchase orders were completed correctly and on time. Our chapter reported to the University of Florida's SHPE chapter (region 6).
