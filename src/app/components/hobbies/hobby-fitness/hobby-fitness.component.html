One thing I have definitely began to prioritize more recently is my health and wellbeing. Doing physical activity has been a great way for me to de-stress and stay healthy while at school.

Nature Walks

Nature Walks

I think talking walks has to be one of my favorite ways to stay active. I usually go to the Nicholas Arboretum on the weekends and take walks and photograph interesting sights as I see them. I usually stay out for hours and really try to connect with nature and clear my mind. I definitely recommend it!

Rock Climbing

Rock Climbing

Coming to Michigan I was able to discover Rock climbing and really found it to be one of my favorite ways to stay active. It is a great way for me to push the limits of all parts of my body. It has also really helped with building confidence in what I can accomplish and helps me focus on my goals, whether that be in my everyday life or in ringing the bell at the top of the wall.

Yoga and Weight Training

Yoga and Weight Training

I put these into one category because I really find that they both build off each other. Yoga has helped me become more flexible and strengthen my muscles against my own body weight. Also the mindset needed for some poses helps me when faced with a stressful situation. The mindset and strength I gain through yoga helps to continue to exert my body when lifting weights at the school gym.

For when I have a busy schedule, these activities make it easy to squeeze in some activity into my day and unwind.
