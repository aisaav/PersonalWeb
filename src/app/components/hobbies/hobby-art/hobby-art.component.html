One of my favorite hobbies is art, I have been painting, sketching and designing since I was five years old. It is a form of creative expression and relaxation for me. I mostly enjoy painting objects with an abnormality about them, at first glance it looks like still-life, but at a closer glance the viewer notices something off about my art. Its intentional as I want to highlight the importance of perspective and analysis in my work. Having a background in art has definitely allowed me to be more creative when solving problems and making my work reflect creativity and elegance.

Oil Painting

Oil Panting

I started oil painting in high school and find it one of my favorite mediums to work with, I have paintings ranging from 15in by 18in and 36in by 48in.

Working with oil is definitely a more challenging medium and it takes more skill to manipulate the paint. I usually begin with a brown wash where I use cloth to quickly determine the brightness of different parts of my painting to create a roadmap for the work. I see this similar to test driven development in my programming where you have to consider the project as a whole before working on the details of implementation.

Charcoal and Sketches

Charcoal and Sketches

I usually work with charcoal and pencil sketching when I dont have access to paint or want to get an idea quickly on paper. Watercolor Painting

Watercolor Painting

As a college student its usually not as easy to purchase materials for oil painting or acrylic, and finding a space to work and let my paintings dry is not as easy. Recently I took a watercolor course to learn more and I absolutely enjoyed it. Now if I ever feel the desire to work with a paint brush, watercolor is an easy medium to use and work with while at school.
